Recontextualizing graphical elements into artistic ones loosen them from their traditional and singular informational role. Once these elements are utilized in an artistic setting, they begin to mesh with their subject and the boundaries between tools of analysis and the analyzed become less defined. The graphical elements are not necessarily placed at random or are relegated merely as numerical or geometric decoration. Their utilization as visual aesthetic elements are partnered with their systematic application within each piece. In my show "Hypervisual" I applied this method to fictionalized and artistic renderings of entomology, anatomy and cartography. Governed by a created system I applied customized infographical elements to my own science fiction. Reds, blacks, and deep grays on a field of a dominating sterile white, push and pull to assert their lines and their solid blocks. The Hypervisual show is a collection of renderings from my make believe world unpacked and examined with my self created method of analysis.
